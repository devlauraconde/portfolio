COYA HOUSE — A Study in Aesthetic Alignment and Visual Harmony

AutoCad SkethUp Lumion Photoshop

The Coya House project represents a masterful balance of form, proportion, and spatial rhythm. Its clean lines, deliberate alignments, and fluid interaction between interior and exterior spaces create an architecture of calm precision.
For me as a web developer, this architectural approach deeply resonates with essential principles of design and front-end development: structure, clarity, and user-focused aesthetics.
Much like a well-organized website layout, the house’s floor plans demonstrate careful attention to alignment and modular consistency. The interplay of open and closed spaces mirrors the way digital interfaces must balance information and whitespace. The minimal material palette and the thoughtful repetition of elements reflect the kind of visual coherence that defines a strong, accessible web experience.
